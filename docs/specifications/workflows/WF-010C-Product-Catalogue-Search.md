# Objective

Implement the dynamic Product Catalogue by integrating the existing Search Platform into the Storefront.

This workflow transforms the existing Search module into the primary customer shopping experience.

The Storefront remains a presentation layer.

No search logic, pricing logic or inventory logic may be duplicated.

The Product Catalogue consumes existing business services through a dedicated Catalog Facade.

# MediaService

Responsibilities

Resolve all storefront media.

Return

- Primary Product Image

# Notes

WF-010C establishes the production Product Catalogue for PCS Core.

The Storefront remains a presentation layer.

Business logic remains within the existing Search, Pricing, Inventory and Catalog services.

The CatalogFacade coordinates all business services.

MediaService provides centralized image resolution.

PageMetadataService provides centralized SEO metadata generation.

StorefrontPresentationService prepares presentation-ready View Models.

Together these services establish the reusable presentation architecture that will also be used by:

- WF-010D Product Detail Experience
- WF-010E Shopping Experience
- Customer Account
- Wishlist
- Brand Pages
- Category Pages
- Future CMS Pages

No further architectural changes to the Storefront should be required after this workflow.
